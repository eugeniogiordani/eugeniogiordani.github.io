This page is dedicated to a story of life and music that began 40 years ago. In 1971 the LOG group was performing in the best clubs in Romagna and Marche. They were 17- to 22-year-olds who loved, like many in those years, pop and rock music.

I would never have imagined that after 40 years those ex-boys could find themselves playing the same music as back then, with the same enthusiasm and joy. In short, a good story.

LOG are back, now of course called LOG 2, and with them also the intriguing harmonies of the vast repertoire of a great musician we have always loved: Brian Auger
